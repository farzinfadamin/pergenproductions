Address:

Centreville, VA 20121

TICKETS WILL BE SOLD ONLINE AND IN ALL PERSIAN STORES FROM MARCH 8TH

Please join us and many others who celebrate Norouz and its 13th day (Sizdah Bedar), again this year at Bull Run Regional Park Special Events Center in Centreville, VA.

Like last year, many people are getting together to bring an organized event and a venue where it can hold more than 10,000 people with FREE PARKING and many amenities to celebrate the ancient Persian picnic day festival.

As we get closer to March 31st, we will provide more details.

This year, you will have:

- Free Parking
- Event Staff
- First Aid Service
- Trash Management
- Persian Food Provided by a variety of vendors
- Numerous Bathrooms
- Kids Playground Including Inflatables and Moon Bounces
- Retailers and Vendors
- Tent and Shades for Advertising
- Tent and Shades for Sitting, Eating, Smoking, Chilling, etc.
- Many Prizes and Drawings
- Traditional Dancers
AND
- Michael and Shahram Shabpareh Live on Stage

DJs: Ani, Iman Barobax, Mehrab, Makan, Shamoudi, and Taba will be there to play music all day for you.

Hosted by ARSHIA and MICHAEL, man of many voices!
